Question 2:

A train 360 m long is running at a speed of 45 km/hr. How long will it take to cross a platform 140 m long?

Answer: D. 40 sec
Explanation: Total distance = 360 + 140 = 500 m
Speed = 45 km/hr = (45 * 1000)/3600 = 12.5 m/sec
Time = 500 / 12.5 = 40 sec
